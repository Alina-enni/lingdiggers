Sometimes you might also need to include quotations.

A longer quote, such as one that spans an entire paragraph, should be done with the blockquote tag. It creates an automatic indentation and nicely sets a quotation apart from the main text. Depending on the style settings in place, it sometimes also reduces the line spacing slightly.
